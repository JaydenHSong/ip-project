# V18 — Missing Required Warnings (필수 경고문 누락)

## Detection Criteria
- Products missing Prop 65 warnings (for California)
- Missing choking hazard warnings for small parts
- Absent battery warnings for lithium-ion products

## Draft Guidelines
- Specify which warning labels are missing
- Reference specific regulatory requirements (Prop 65, CPSIA)
- Note the product characteristics requiring warnings
- Request addition of required warning text

## Evidence Requirements
- Product listing showing absence of required warnings
- Regulatory requirement for the specific warning
- Product characteristics triggering warning requirements

## Common Patterns
- Missing California Prop 65 cancer/reproductive harm warnings
- No choking hazard labels on products with small parts
- Battery products without proper lithium-ion warnings
