# V10 — Variation Policy Violation (Variation 정책 위반)

## Detection Criteria
- Unrelated products grouped as variations
- Variations used to inherit reviews from different products
- Incorrect variation relationships (size/color abuse)

## Draft Guidelines
- Describe the variation abuse pattern
- Identify which products are incorrectly linked
- Reference Amazon's Variation Relationship Policy
- Explain review manipulation aspect if applicable

## Evidence Requirements
- Screenshots showing variation relationships
- Evidence that products are fundamentally different
- Review count anomalies indicating review hijacking

## Common Patterns
- Phone cases for different models grouped as color variations
- Accessories bundled with unrelated products as variations
- Old ASINs revived as variations of popular listings
