# V09 — Comparative Advertising (타 브랜드 비교 광고)

## Detection Criteria
- Direct comparison with Spigen products in listing content
- Use of Spigen brand name in comparison charts or images
- Disparaging claims about Spigen products

## Draft Guidelines
- Quote the comparative advertising content
- Explain how it violates Amazon's comparison policy
- Note any disparaging or misleading comparisons
- Reference Amazon's guidelines on comparative claims

## Evidence Requirements
- Screenshots of comparative content
- Specific text or images making the comparison

## Common Patterns
- "Better than Spigen" claims
- Side-by-side comparison images using Spigen products
- Feature comparison tables mentioning Spigen by name
