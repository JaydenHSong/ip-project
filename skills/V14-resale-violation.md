# V14 — Resale Violation (재판매 위반)

## Detection Criteria
- Unauthorized resellers of Spigen products
- Products sold outside authorized distribution channels
- Grey market goods with warranty implications

## Draft Guidelines
- Identify the unauthorized reseller
- Reference Spigen's authorized distribution policy
- Note potential consumer impact (warranty, authenticity)
- Reference Amazon's policies on authorized sellers

## Evidence Requirements
- Seller information and storefront
- Evidence of unauthorized status
- Product condition concerns if applicable

## Common Patterns
- Sellers not in Spigen's authorized dealer list
- Products shipped from unauthorized regions
- Repackaged or refurbished items sold as new
