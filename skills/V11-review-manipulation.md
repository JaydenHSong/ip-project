# V11 — Review Manipulation / Incentivized Reviews (리뷰 조작 / 인센티브 리뷰)

## Detection Criteria
- Abnormal review patterns (bulk positive reviews in short period)
- Reviews mentioning free products or discounts for reviews
- Reviewer profiles showing patterns of incentivized reviews

## Draft Guidelines
- Document the review pattern anomaly with dates and counts
- Quote specific reviews indicating manipulation
- Reference Amazon's Community Guidelines on reviews
- Include statistical evidence of review anomalies

## Evidence Requirements
- Screenshots of suspicious reviews
- Review date/count analysis
- Reviewer profile patterns if detectable

## Common Patterns
- Sudden spike of 5-star reviews within days
- Reviews containing similar language or templates
- Reviewers with history of reviewing similar products
