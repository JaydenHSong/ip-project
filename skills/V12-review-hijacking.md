# V12 — Review Hijacking (리뷰 하이재킹)

## Detection Criteria
- Reviews referencing a completely different product
- Product listing changed after accumulating reviews
- Reviews mentioning features not present in current listing

## Draft Guidelines
- Document mismatch between reviews and current product
- Quote reviews that clearly reference different products
- Show timeline of listing changes if available
- Reference Amazon's listing abuse policies

## Evidence Requirements
- Screenshots of mismatched reviews
- Product listing content showing different product from reviews
- Historical listing data if available

## Common Patterns
- Reviews mentioning different product category
- Reviews with photos showing different product
- Listing title/description changed from original product
