# V03 — Patent Infringement (특허 침해)

## Detection Criteria
- Products incorporating Spigen's patented design elements
- Utility patent violations in product construction or mechanism
- Design patent similarities in product appearance

## Draft Guidelines
- Reference specific patent numbers from Spigen's patent registry
- Describe which claims are being infringed
- Include visual comparison for design patents
- Cite Amazon's patent infringement reporting procedures

## Evidence Requirements
- Patent registration numbers and claims
- Product images showing patent similarity
- Technical comparison of patented features vs infringing product
- Patent similarity analysis results

## Common Patterns
- Kickstand mechanisms matching Spigen design patents
- Air cushion technology replicas
- Case design profiles matching patented designs
