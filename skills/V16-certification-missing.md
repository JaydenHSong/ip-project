# V16 — Missing Certifications (인증 미비 — FCC/UL 등)

## Detection Criteria
- Electronic products without required FCC certification
- Products claiming certifications they don't hold
- Missing UL or other safety certifications for applicable products

## Draft Guidelines
- Identify the missing certification requirement
- Reference specific regulatory requirements
- Note potential safety risks to consumers
- Cite Amazon's compliance requirements

## Evidence Requirements
- Product listing showing electronic/certified product claims
- Evidence of missing certification
- Regulatory requirement references

## Common Patterns
- Wireless charging products without FCC ID
- Battery products without UL certification
- Products claiming CE marking without documentation
