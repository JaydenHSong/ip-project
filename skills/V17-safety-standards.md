# V17 — Safety Standard Non-compliance (안전 기준 미달)

## Detection Criteria
- Products failing to meet applicable safety standards
- Materials or construction posing safety risks
- Products recalled or flagged by safety agencies

## Draft Guidelines
- Identify specific safety standard violations
- Reference CPSC or relevant safety agency requirements
- Describe potential consumer safety risks
- Request immediate listing removal if imminent danger

## Evidence Requirements
- Product information showing safety concerns
- Applicable safety standard references
- Safety agency notifications if available

## Common Patterns
- Phone cases with materials exceeding chemical limits
- Screen protectors with glass breakage hazards
- Products with small parts not labeled for choking hazard
