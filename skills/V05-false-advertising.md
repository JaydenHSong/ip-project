# V05 — False/Misleading Advertising (허위/과장 광고)

## Detection Criteria
- Unsubstantiated claims about product performance or features
- Misleading compatibility claims with devices
- False certifications or endorsements

## Draft Guidelines
- Quote specific misleading claims from the listing
- Explain why the claims are false or unsubstantiated
- Reference Amazon's Product Detail Page Rules
- Suggest specific corrections needed

## Evidence Requirements
- Screenshots of false/misleading claims
- Evidence disproving the claims if available
- Amazon policy references

## Common Patterns
- "Military grade" or "MIL-STD" claims without certification
- Exaggerated drop protection ratings
- False compatibility with specific device models
