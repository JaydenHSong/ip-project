# V15 — Bundling Violation (번들링 위반)

## Detection Criteria
- Unauthorized bundling of Spigen products with other items
- Bundles that mislead consumers about included items
- Bundles created to manipulate product rankings

## Draft Guidelines
- Describe the unauthorized bundle composition
- Explain how it misleads consumers
- Reference Amazon's Product Bundling Policy
- Note impact on Spigen brand perception

## Evidence Requirements
- Screenshot of bundle listing
- Evidence of unauthorized combination
- Comparison with legitimate Spigen offerings

## Common Patterns
- Spigen case bundled with cheap screen protectors
- Multiple Spigen products bundled without authorization
- Non-Spigen products bundled claiming Spigen compatibility
