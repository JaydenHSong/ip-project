# V13 — Price Manipulation (가격 조작)

## Detection Criteria
- Artificially inflated reference prices for fake discounts
- Predatory pricing to drive out competitors
- Price gouging during high-demand periods

## Draft Guidelines
- Document the pricing discrepancy with evidence
- Compare with market average pricing
- Reference Amazon's Fair Pricing Policy
- Include historical pricing data if available

## Evidence Requirements
- Screenshot of current pricing
- Historical price data or comparison
- Market average pricing reference

## Common Patterns
- "Was $99.99, Now $9.99" with inflated original price
- Consistent below-cost pricing suggesting dumping
- Price spikes during product launches or shortages
