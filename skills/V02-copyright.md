# V02 — Copyright Infringement / Image Theft (저작권 침해 / 이미지 도용)

## Detection Criteria
- Use of Spigen's proprietary product images without authorization
- Copying Spigen's A+ Content or Enhanced Brand Content
- Reproduction of Spigen lifestyle/marketing images

## Draft Guidelines
- Identify specific images that are copied with URLs or screenshots
- Reference original Spigen listing where images first appeared
- Cite DMCA and Amazon's copyright infringement policies
- Request immediate removal of infringing content

## Evidence Requirements
- Side-by-side comparison of original vs infringing images
- Original image source (Spigen listing ASIN)
- Copyright registration if available

## Common Patterns
- Direct copy of main product images
- Cropped or slightly modified versions of Spigen photos
- Reuse of infographic or comparison chart images
