# V07 — Inaccurate Product Information (부정확한 상품 정보)

## Detection Criteria
- Wrong product specifications (dimensions, weight, materials)
- Incorrect device compatibility information
- Mismatched product category listing

## Draft Guidelines
- Specify which information is inaccurate with evidence
- Provide correct information for comparison
- Reference Amazon's Product Detail Page policies
- Focus on consumer confusion and safety risks

## Evidence Requirements
- Screenshots of inaccurate information
- Correct specifications for comparison
- Customer complaints about inaccuracy if available

## Common Patterns
- Wrong device model compatibility
- Incorrect material descriptions
- Misleading product dimensions or weight
