# V04 — Counterfeit Products (위조품 판매)

## Detection Criteria
- Products claiming to be genuine Spigen but sold by unauthorized sellers
- Listings using Spigen branding with suspiciously low prices
- Products with incorrect packaging, logos, or quality indicators

## Draft Guidelines
- Clearly state the product is counterfeit/not genuine Spigen
- Reference Amazon's Anti-Counterfeiting Policy
- Include price comparison showing abnormally low pricing
- Note unauthorized seller status via Brand Registry

## Evidence Requirements
- Listing screenshot with pricing and seller information
- Brand Registry enrollment proof
- Price comparison with genuine Spigen products
- Customer reviews mentioning quality issues if available

## Common Patterns
- Prices significantly below Spigen MSRP (>50% discount)
- Sellers with no authorization from Spigen
- Product images that look genuine but from unauthorized source
